About Mathe

Our mission is to provide free, high-quality math education to learners worldwide.

Our Mission

Mathe was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to make quality math education accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or economic status. We believe that education is a fundamental right, and we're committed to breaking down barriers to learning.

Our platform supports SDG 4: Quality Education by providing free, inclusive, and equitable educational resources that promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

  • • Ensure equal access to affordable technical, vocational and higher education
  • • Substantially increase the number of youth and adults who have relevant skills for employment
  • • Eliminate gender disparities in education and ensure equal access for the vulnerable

What Makes Us Different

Quality Content

All our materials are created by experienced educators and follow established curricula.

Community Focus

We build connections between students and teachers to create a supportive learning environment.

Global Access

Our platform is designed to be accessible from anywhere, even with limited internet connectivity.

Continuous Improvement

We regularly update our content based on feedback and educational research.
